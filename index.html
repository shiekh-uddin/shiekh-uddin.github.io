About Me

I'm currently a postdoc at MIT working with Professor Marin Soljačić. I recieved my PhD from UC Berkeley in Professor Ali Javey's lab in 2022, where I also collaborated heavily with Professor Eli Yablonovitch and Professor Eran Rabani. I have been a Lab Manager/Activity Lead/Principal Safety Officer at Material Science Division, Lawrence Berkely National Lab. In my PhD I explored photophysics of low dimensional materials. I received my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from BUET (Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology), where I worked on digital signal processing and nanophotonics. In my free time, I play around with micro and macro photography, read history of science or autobiographies, and observe wildlife.
