Research Interests

  • Defect-tolerant optoelectronics: Defects in conventional semiconductors drastically lower their electronic and optoelectronic performance. However excitonic semiconductors exhibit dramatic resilience to imperfections and can show near perfect optoelectronic performance even in the presence of large defect density. I explore the influence and manipulation of complex Coloumb interaction for superior classical light emitting devices.
  • Nonclassical light emitters: In the advent of the quantum revolution, it is imperative to develop light sources with nonclassical properties. I explore elements of photonics, nonlinear optics, quantum optics, and material science to develop nonclassical light emitters.
