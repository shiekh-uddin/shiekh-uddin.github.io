About Me

I'm currently a postdoc at MIT working with Professor Marin Soljačić. I recieved my PhD from UC Berkeley in Professor Ali Javey's lab in 2022. I have also been a Lab Manager/ Activity Lead/ Principal Safety Officer at Material Science Division, Lawrence Berkely National Lab. I explore photophysics of low dimensional materials in my academic research. Previously I have worked on digital signal processing and nanophotonics. I received my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from BUET (Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology). In my free time, I play around with micro and macro photography, read history of science or autobiographies.
